About:
Side Project Generator


Hello and welcom to Side Project
Generator dot come! Do you ever find
yourself running out of creative
stamina? Are you looking for a fun and
interactive way to come up with new ideas for your business or personal
gain? Well then, you've come to the
right place! Simply click on Stephen
Colbert to bring up two random topics.
Then ask, how could these two things be
combined to create something awesome?
Side project, real project, vegetarian
project. Use it for anything! If you
really like one topic, but not the
other, just click the lock on the side
you fancy and keep going! If you need
new ideas for a brand or product, write
that brand or product on a Post-It note
and stick it on one side of the screen.
Not every pair will be a winner, but
remember, you're a creative!
Anything is possible!


By Nick Rog and Russ Rizzo, with Alex Nelson
